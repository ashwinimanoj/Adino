About Us

    Adino Consulting Pvt Ltd is the most prominent and competent human resource services, delivering a broad range of human resource services to various industries. Passion is everything in recruitment. Passionate recruiter evolves from being a good recruiter to a great recruiter.

     Recruiters must be passionate about their job, the company, and the candidates. And, great recruiters are passionate about getting results. We take a consultative approach to recruiting, screening, and placing quality talent. Our exceptional Team specialises in Permanent placements for Small to Medium size Businesses and we will walk you through the entire process from start to finish, saving you time, money and taking the pain from the Recruitment process and connecting you with the perfect fit for your Team. As an employer, taking the time to make the right placement, will help with the growth and financial success of your Business.

    We concentrate on delivering continued success when it comes to staff retention, satisfaction and suitability. We want to have a long term relationship with your business, and that means understanding your business; past, present and future. Our consultants are encouraged to add value to our clients by investing time into a thorough and structured recruitment process. A successful long term employee and employer relationship is more than just matching skills with job responsibilities.

     Company culture, personal goals and values must align and this can only occur when understanding our clients the way we do in Adino. We offer a range of Professional Services that is tailor made to suit your individual needs and company expectations!
